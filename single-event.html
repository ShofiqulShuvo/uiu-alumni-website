Seminar on Success Σeet: Academia to Industry

Published on: Jun 25, 2022
Venue: Smart Room-126
Event Dates: Jun 30, 2022

We are excited to invite you to "Success Σeet: Academia to Industry", a seminar organized by the UIU Robotics Club and led by Mr. Furkan Alahi, our esteemed CSE alumnus. Mr. Furkan is currently working as a Software Engineer at Delinian in London, UK.
This event will provide essential guidance on navigating the competitive software and robotics job market, including insights on trends, resume tips, interviews, soft skills, and entering the global job market.

Event Details:

Date: 23rd June 2024

Time: 2:00 PM Onwards

Venue: Room 126, Smart Room

Registration Link: Success Σeet Form Link

Event Link: Facebook Event Link

Please bring a soft copy of your CV (without your name) for personalized feedback on how to improve it. Your presence will greatly contribute to the success of the seminar. We look forward to seeing you there!!
